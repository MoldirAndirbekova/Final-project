Ingredients

Carbonated Water, Organic Blood Orange Juice, Calamansi Juice, Organic Agave Syrup, Jalapeño Extract, Smoked Salt, Mexican Lime Extract

RECIPES

A lighter spicy Marg

1.5 oz. of Tequila or Mezcal
4 oz. of AVEC Jalapeño & Blood Orange (or, half a can)
Lime Wedge

In a glass, add ice. Pour spirit over ice. Top off with AVEC. Gently stir. Add a squeeze of lime and garnish.

Note: for best results, chill your AVEC before using.
