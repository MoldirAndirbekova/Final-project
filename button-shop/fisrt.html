THE SAMPLER

12 Pack of 8.45 oz Cans 28 Reviews
The Sampler gives you the chance to experience 5 AVEC flavors. Inside our little green box of drinks, you'll find 3 cans of the following flavors:

• Yuzu & Lime
• Grapefruit & Pomelo
• Jalapeño & Blood Orange
• Hibiscus & Pomegranate
• Ginger

Each box is a total of 15 cans — all of which have less than 6 grams of sugar and can be mixed with a spirit or sipped solo for a non-alc treat.
