Avec [Av-ek] is French for “with”. Because what you mix with your alcohol matters. Nobody's denying the Paloma is a classic cocktail, but it's full of sugar and begging for an update. By mixing two member of the grapefruit family - ruby and pomelo - and blending them with a hint of black pepper and vanilla, we have made this vintage tipple fresh again. 15 calories, 4 g sugar – more nutrition details below!

A Paloma All Without Sugar

1.5 oz. of something or Mezcal
4 oz. of AVEC Jalapeño & Blood Orange (or, half a can)

In a glass, add ice. Pour spirit over ice. Top off with AVEC. Gently stir. Add a squeeze of lime and garnish.

Note: for best results, chill your AVEC before using.
